
DAX --> data analysis expressions

#----------------------------------------------------------------

DAX is used for two things ..

Calculated Columns: Computed row-by-row and stored in the table.

Measures: Computed on the fly based on what the user clicks in the report.

#----------------------------------------------------------------

Types of DAX Functions

These are the basics. They take a column and turn it into a single number.

SUM(): Adds everything up.
AVERAGE(): Finds the mean.
COUNT() / DISTINCTCOUNT(): Counts rows or unique values (like counting how many unique Customers you had).
